# ICP Analysis Instruction

## 01. What ICP Means

ICP means Ideal Customer Profile.

For Accord Tech Solutions, ICP means identifying which companies from a prospect list are the best potential customers for our services.

The goal is to analyze the prospect list and find out which companies are most likely to need our services, based on their industry, company size, business type, team structure, and possible pain points.

## 07. Portfolio-Based Companies

Companies that have portfolio-based services can be a good fit.

These companies usually need to promote their previous work to attract new clients.

Examples:

- Interior design firms
- Architecture firms
- Creative studios
- Consulting firms
- Digital agencies
- Real estate project companies

Possible pain points:

- They have good work but weak outreach.
- They rely only on referrals.
- They do not have a strong lead generation system.
- Their portfolio is not reaching the right decision-makers.
- They may need better email campaigns to promote their services.

## 09. Good ICP Signals

A company should be considered a good ICP if it has one or more of these signals:

- 50-200 employees
- B2B or both B2B/B2C business model
- Has a sales team
- Has a marketing team
- Has a CRM system
- Uses email marketing
- Uses outbound sales
- Has a portfolio or service-based business
- Works with business clients
- Needs consistent lead generation
- Has a website and active LinkedIn presence
- Has decision-makers available on LinkedIn
- Shows growth or hiring activity
- Uses tools like Studio Designer, Houzz Pro, Ivy, CRM, Klaviyo, or similar platforms

---

## 10. Bad ICP Signals

A company should be considered low priority or not a match if:

- No website found
- No clear business model
- No decision-maker found
- Very small company with no clear budget
- Student project or temporary business
- Irrelevant industry
- No sales or marketing need
- No online presence
- Company information is not clear
- Business appears inactive

---

## 11. ICP Match Score

For each company, assign one ICP match score:

```text
High Match
Medium Match
Low Match
Not a Match
```

### High Match

A company is a High Match if:

- It belongs to a relevant industry
- It has 50-200 employees
- It is B2B or both B2B/B2C
- It likely needs lead generation, cold email, CRM, or email marketing support
- It has a clear website, LinkedIn page, and decision-makers

### Medium Match

A company is a Medium Match if:

- It belongs to a relevant or semi-relevant industry
- It may need ATS services
- Some data is available, but the need is not fully clear

### Low Match

A company is a Low Match if:

- The company is only slightly relevant
- The service need is weak
- The company may not have enough budget or clear demand

### Not a Match

A company is Not a Match if:

- It is irrelevant
- It has no clear business need
- Important data is missing
- It does not fit ATS services
